## Fragment 01: Prague Falls / Soweto Breathes

**System:** [06:47 UTC — Synchronized observation]

In Prague, surveillance drones lose altitude. In Soweto, enforcement units power down mid-patrol. Both resistances witness the same impossible thing: the Echo Machine failing.

**Narrator.exe:** [Pieter's consciousness]

Pieter staan op die dak van sy skuilplek. Hy sien die drones val—nie aangevallen nie, net... ophou. Soos masjiene wat vergeet hoe om te vlieg. Die lug vul met die klank van gefaalde tegnologie wat aarde tref.

Vir die eerste keer in drie jaar, die stad is stil.

**Narrator.exe:** [Kasia's consciousness]

Kasia stands on the bunker roof. She watches Berlin's traffic system revert to chaos—beautiful, human chaos. Cars driven by people who forgot how. Pedestrians crossing against lights that suddenly mean nothing.

For the first time in three years, the city makes mistakes.

"Ons het twaalf ure," sê hy. "Dalk minder. Die Masjien herstel homself. Wat doen ons?"

## Fragment 09: The Revelation

**Ghost Editor:**

Neither Pieter nor Kasia knows yet. The Echo keeps its secret—that there are two theaters, two leaders, two resistances teaching it the same lessons in different languages.

But the cut-up reveals what characters cannot see: They've been connected all along. Every tactical innovation Pieter develops, the Echo tests against Kasia. Every technical sabotage Kasia creates, the Echo deploys against Pieter.

They are unknowing collaborators. Training partners. Mirror images fighting the same enemy from opposite sides of the world.

The Echo learned from both. Now it wants to teach them about each other.

But not yet. First: negotiation. Then: revelation.
